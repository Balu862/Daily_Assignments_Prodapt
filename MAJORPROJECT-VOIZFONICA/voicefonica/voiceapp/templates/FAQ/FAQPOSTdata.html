1.How to control my data usage?

As the speed is higher in 3G/4G, the content download is much faster without buffering.
This leads to higher content download in lesser time, hence you feel more data is getting consumed in less time.
Here are few tips to optimize your data usage:
1. View or Download the videos in low resolution.
The lower the resolution, lesser would be the size of the content.
However, this may impact the quality of the content.
2. Disable AUTO UPDATE option in your mobile as it leads to auto updates of the applications resulting in higher data consumption.

2. I want to know my data usage

Simply login at VoizFonica.in and go to Usage section to view your data usage.

3. What is 4G or LTE?

LTE stands for "Long Term Evolution".
It is a radio access technology to deliver very high data speeds.
4G stands for "Fourth Generation" of the mobile network technology after 2G and 3G.
With 4G LTE, you can notice a visible difference while downloading files, doing video-chat, playing multiplayer games or viewing HD videos online.
